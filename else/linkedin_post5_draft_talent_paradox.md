📉 The 2026 Tech Talent Paradox in one sentence:
"There are thousands of developers looking for jobs, but I still can't hire a Senior Cloud Architect."

We hear this from CTOs every week. 
The market is flooded with junior talent, but the demand for senior, specialized engineers (AI, DevSecOps, Cloud-native) is severely outpacing supply in Europe and the US.

When a senior seat sits empty for 4 months, you don't just lose time. You lose:
❌ Roadmap momentum
❌ Your current team's morale (hello, burnout)
❌ Market share to faster competitors

If you're limiting your search to a 50km radius of your office, the paradox will only get worse.

Meanwhile, Vietnam’s IT workforce has grown to over 560,000 professionals, with a massive surge in senior talent specializing in modern stacks.

You don't need a hiring miracle. You need a broader radius.
