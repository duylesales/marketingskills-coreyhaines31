🏗️ Your legacy system is costing you money every day.

But should you refactor it... or rebuild from scratch?

Here's the decision framework we use at Manifera after 160+ software projects:

[CAROUSEL CONTENT]
**Slide 1:** Refactor or Rebuild? The Decision Framework
**Slide 2:** Refactor When...
- The codebase is < 5 years old
- Core architecture is modular
- Budget & timeline are tight
- Original devs are still on the team
- Risk tolerance is low
**Slide 3:** Rebuild When...
- The tech stack is obsolete
- Security flaws are structural
- Maintenance cost > Rebuild cost
- Original devs are gone, no docs
- Competitors are shipping 2x faster
**Slide 4:** The Hidden 3rd Option: Strangler Fig Pattern
Don't want a risky "Big Bang" rebuild? Gradually replace your monolith piece by piece using microservices while the legacy app keeps running.
**Slide 5:** Cost Comparison (2026 Data)
Refactor: $50K-$200K (2-6 months)
Rebuild: $200K-$1M+ (6-18 months)
*Costs based on typical enterprise apps.*
